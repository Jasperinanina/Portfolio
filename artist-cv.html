Jasper Nollet is a Ghent-based artist exploring humanity’s future as an interplanetary species, and how this shift might reshape our technology and collective identity. Fascinated by the overview effect; the astronaut’s sense of unity when viewing Earth from space. He merges ancient archetypes with futuristic forms, creating speculative dialogues between the past we inherit and the future we imagine.

Jasper Nollet is a Ghent-based artist exploring humanity’s future as an interplanetary species, and how this shift might reshape our technology and collective identity. Fascinated by the overview effect; the astronaut’s sense of unity when viewing Earth from space. He merges ancient archetypes with futuristic forms, creating speculative dialogues between the past we inherit and the future we imagine.
